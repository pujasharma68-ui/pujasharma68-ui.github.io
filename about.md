# About Me

I am a content writer transitioning into technical writing with a strong foundation in marketing communication, content strategy, and user-focused writing.

My experience in marketing content has helped me develop the ability to simplify complex information, understand audience needs, and create clear, engaging, and structured content. While working on different forms of content such as blogs, presentations, webpages, and case studies, I developed a strong interest in documentation and instructional writing.

To strengthen my transition into technical writing, I have actively learned and practiced:

- Technical documentation
- User guides and onboarding content
- Release notes
- Concept and task-based writing
- Markdown and Docs-as-Code workflows
- Documentation structuring
- UX-focused communication

Through self-learning and hands-on writing samples, I have built a portfolio that demonstrates my ability to communicate technical information in a clear, organized, and user-friendly way.
